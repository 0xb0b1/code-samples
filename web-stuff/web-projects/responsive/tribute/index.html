Chester Bennington

The man who everyone loved.

Chest Charles Bennington was an American singer, songwriter, musician, and actor.

Here's a timeline of Chester Bennington:"

  • 1976: Born in Phoenix, Arizona, U.S.
  • 1977: His parents got divorced when he was 11 years old.
  • 1993: Bennington began singing with a band called Sean Dowdell and His Friends? and together they released an eponymous three-track cassette.
  • 1994: He joined a new band, Grey Daze, a post-grunge band from Phoenix, Arizona. The band recorded a Demo in 1993 and two albums: Wake Me in 1994, and ...No Sun Today in 1997.
  • 1998: Bennington left Grey Daze.
  • 2000: Chester Bennington was in a band called Linkin Park, their've release their debut album, Hybrid Theory, through Warner Bros. Records.
  • 2002: Bennington later described the songwriting experience to Rolling Stone magazine. "It's easy to fall into that thing – 'poor, poor me', that's where songs like 'Crawling' come from: I can't take myself. But that song is about taking responsibility for your actions. I don't say 'you' at any point. It's about how I'm the reason that I feel this way. There's something inside me that pulls me down."
